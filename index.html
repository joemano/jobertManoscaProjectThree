Instructions

  • Pick a Color

    Choose one of the colors on the right. The selected color is highlighted for you.

  • Match the Color

    Click or tap on a region of the corresponding letter to fill the paint.

  • Complete the Image

    Fill all the regions to complete the image. Completing the image unlocks the next page.

Tip: Remember to pinch the screen to zoom in and out!
